About

Beabadoobee, whose real name is Beatrice Laus, is a captivating artist who has captivated audiences with her unique blend of indie pop and rock music. Born in 2000 in the Philippines and raised in London, Beabadoobee discovered her love for music at an early age, inspired by the likes of Pavement, The Beatles, and Elliot Smith. With her distinctive vocals and introspective songwriting, she has quickly risen to prominence in the music industry. Beabadoobee gained significant recognition with her breakout hit "Coffee" in 2017, which showcased her raw talent and lyrical prowess. Since then, she has continued to evolve as an artist, releasing critically acclaimed projects like her debut album "Fake It Flowers" in 2020. Beabadoobee's music effortlessly captures the essence of youth and nostalgia, resonating with listeners on a deeply emotional level. With her mesmerizing sound and undeniable talent, Beabadoobee is undoubtedly an artist to watch, leaving an indelible mark on the music scene.
